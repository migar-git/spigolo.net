Shipping Information

Premium artisan pasta delivered from Italy to your door

International Shipping

All Spigolo orders ship directly from Italy, where our artisan pasta is made. We partner with trusted international carriers to ensure your pasta arrives in perfect condition.

Delivery Times

United States & Canada: 5-10 business days
European Union: 3-7 business days
United Kingdom: 5-10 business days
Australia & New Zealand: 7-14 business days

Note: Delivery times are estimates and may vary during peak seasons or due to customs clearance.

Shipping Costs

Free shipping on all orders over $75 USD (or equivalent in your local currency).

For orders under $75:

  • United States & Canada: $12 flat rate
  • European Union: €10 flat rate
  • United Kingdom: £9 flat rate
  • Australia & New Zealand: $18 AUD flat rate

Packaging

We take great care in packaging your order to ensure safe arrival:

  • Each package is individually wrapped and cushioned
  • Boxes are reinforced to protect against handling
  • Gift boxes include premium presentation packaging
  • All packaging materials are recyclable or compostable

Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can monitor your shipment's progress from Italy to your doorstep.

Customs & Import Duties

International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes in your country. These fees are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our shipping costs. Most food items under $100-200 USD typically clear customs without additional fees, but regulations vary by country.

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. During peak seasons (holidays, special promotions), processing may take 2-3 business days.

Temperature-Controlled Shipping

Dried pasta is shelf-stable and does not require refrigeration. However, we avoid shipping to destinations with extreme heat during summer months. If you're in a hot climate, we'll hold your order until temperatures moderate or contact you with options.

Delivery Issues

If your order doesn't arrive within the estimated timeframe, or if your package arrives damaged, please contact us at ciao@spigolo.net within 48 hours. We'll work with you to resolve the issue promptly.

Address Accuracy

Please ensure your shipping address is complete and accurate. We cannot be responsible for orders shipped to incorrect addresses provided at checkout. Address changes must be requested within 4 hours of order placement.

Holiday Shipping

During major holidays, we recommend placing orders at least 2-3 weeks in advance to ensure timely delivery. Check our homepage for specific holiday shipping deadlines.

Questions?

If you have questions about shipping or your order, please visit our FAQ page or email us at ciao@spigolo.net. We typically respond within 24 hours.
