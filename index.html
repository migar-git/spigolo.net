The Spigolo Standard

How we source the world's finest pasta

01

Bronze-Die Extrusion

We only work with pastai who use traditional bronze dies. This creates a rough, porous surface that grips sauce beautifully — unlike Teflon dies used by industrial producers.

02

Slow Drying

Our pasta is dried naturally over 24 to 72 hours at low temperatures. This preserves the wheat's complex flavors and nutrients. Mass-market pasta? Dried in 2 hours at high heat.

03

Heritage Wheat

We seek out makers who use ancient grain varieties and organic Italian durum wheat. These heirloom wheats have deeper flavor and superior texture.

04

Family Tradition

Every producer we partner with is a multi-generational family business. They're not just making pasta — they're preserving a centuries-old craft.
